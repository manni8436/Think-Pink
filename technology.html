Technology Advancements

How technology plays a part in the fight

As the fight against cancer evolves, so too do the weapons that are used. Technology has become a crucial part in the journeys that patients and medical professionals take through each step of the way.

Below are just a few examples of the technologies that are available - from early detection to screening to treatment.

Thermaiscan

Thermaiscan is a HealthTech company focusing on predictive medicine, founded in Stockholm, Sweden. They have developed a tool that finds abnormalities in breast tissue that can be a sign of early or developed stages of breast cancer.

Learn More

'Breast Aware' App

Knowledge and early detection are key. The earlier women know what is normal for them, if an abnormality should arise it will be detected and treated quickly. This app sets monthly check reminders & provides a step-by-step video walkthrough for checking at home.

Learn More

TNM Cancer Staging App

The TNM Cancer Staging App is a mobile app that provides navigable information to help physicians determine the level of treatment and prognosis for cancer patients. The app condenses a 1000-page book and puts it into the hands of patients, doctors and practitioners.
