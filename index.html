Who We Are

We are team DD-AA, proudly representing women and men of all sizes!
Our goal is to raise awareness of the symptoms of Breast Cancer and its impact on those affected and to showcase recent technological advances in the fight against it.

Survivor Stories

These stories of brave survivors of breast cancer are included to inspire and encourage others who find themselves in similar circumstances. They are about growing awareness of the reality of another's suffering. Their aim is to enable us to gain respect and compassion for the others, as human beings, and as survivors of breast cancer.
